🗑️ Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the app:
Settings → Delete Account.

This permanently removes your profile, activities, and all personal data. If you're unable to access the app, email us at support@kadam.fitness and we will delete your account manually within 7 days.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the Login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

Why isn't GPS tracking starting?

Make sure Kadam has Location permission set to Always in your device's Settings → Privacy → Location Services. Also ensure you're outdoors or near a window for a faster GPS fix.

How do I change my date of birth?

Go to Settings → Edit Date of Birth. A change request will be submitted for review.

My account was suspended — what do I do?

If you believe the suspension was a mistake, use the appeal form shown on the suspended screen, or email us at support@kadam.fitness with your registered email address.

How do I report another user?

Tap the three-dot menu on any post or profile and select Report. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
